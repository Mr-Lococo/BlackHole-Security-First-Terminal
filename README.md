# More:
## Pluggable tools architecture?
#### What exactly do we mean when we say pluggable tools?
- We mean that you the client can make, buy or sell your own tools and help the community and us out.
- we don't have 3rd party site, so be careful scams or other threats ( only our own site and discord server.)
## Minimal Install footprint?
#### What exactly do we mean when we say Minimal Install Footprint?
- we mean that us(BlackHole) give you a 'empty' box that you can install tools that will actually help you instead of causing bloatware.
- that will help with system resources and disk space.
## Custom Protection Rules and modes?
#### What exactly do we mean when we say Custom Protection Rules and modes?
- we mean that you can ether chose one of our security level modes or customize your own rules.
- we have 3 levels of security for you to try and decide( or make your own )
## Custom scripting modules?
#### What exactly do we mean when we say Custom scripting modules?
- we mean that you can script your own tool/s for *BlackHole* and share them
- Remember, we recommend taking a look at the [LICENSE](LICENSE). Because your tool will also need to be a AGPL v3.
---
## Tool Monetization Module:
Q: Can i create, sell or share tools for *BlackHole*?
- A: yes. Creators can build and monetize tools. Here's the process:
1. **Submit your tool** via ticket on discord.
2. **Security audit** we scan for malware and verify AGPL v3 compliance.
3. **Listed in marketplace** available on discord and website once approved
---
Q: Does it cost any money to create/publish tools?
- A: No. we don't take money for creating/publishing tools but BlackHole takes 5-10% on paid tools only. Free tools are always free to publish
---
Q: It is safe to get tools?
- A: yes, we check for malware before it reaches on the marketplace, even so all tools run isolated, so even if something slips through, it can't escape your system.
